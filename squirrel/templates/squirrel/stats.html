Squirrel Figures

What numbers tell us about the squirrels of Central Park


  • 3018 squirrels
  • 9:1 adult : juvenile ratio
  • 83% of the squirrels have grey fur
  • black fur squirrels are very rare, only 103 squirrels have black fur
  • most common activity is foraging, almost half of the sightings
  • only 22% of the sightings reported climbing activity
  • 45% vs. 55% morning vs. afternoon/evening sightings ratio
